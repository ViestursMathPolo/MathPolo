MathPolo is a growing repository of benchmark-style mathematics problems (and future physics problems) and structured solutions for AI math training, STEM reasoning evaluation, and LLM benchmark development.

A well-solved problem does more than produce an answer — it reveals structure, reasoning, and a deeper secret of the world.

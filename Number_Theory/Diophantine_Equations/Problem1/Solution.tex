{\bf Solution to Problem 1}

Rearrange the equation as

\begin{equation}
a=\frac{r^2-2}{3}.
\end{equation}

Try $r=3n, 3n+1, 3n+2$, $n\in N$.

(a) $r=3n$.


$r^2-2=9n^2-2$. Not divisible by 3 - solution with $r=3n$ not possible.

(b) $r=3n+1$.

$r^2-2=(3n+1)^2-2=9n^2+6n+1-2=9n^2+6n-1$. Not divisible by 3 - solution with $r=3n+1$ not possible.

(c) $r=3n+2$.

$r^2-2=(3n+2)^2-2=9n^2+6n+4-2=9n^2+6n+2$. Not divisible by 3 - solution with $r=3n+2$ not possible.
 
After exhausting all possible cases, we conclude that there are no integer solutions.

Note: $r^2=3a-2$ has integer solutions with $r=3n+1$ and $3n+2$, but not with $3n$.
